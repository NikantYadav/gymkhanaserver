How to Post Complaints on the CEMMC Portal

1

Open the CEMMC Complaints Portal

Click the link below to access the CEMMC complaints portal for IITK:
Go to the CEMMC Portal
2

Log in with Your CC ID and Password

Use your CC ID and Password to log in. Click on Login Based Services to open the login form.
3

Navigate to the Complaints Section

After logging in, you will see the main dashboard. Look for the "CEMMC - Complaint Management System" section under the services listed. Click on it to proceed.
Click on "Complaint Mgmt Online" in the sidebar, and select File a Complaint.
4

Fill in Your Complaint Details

In the complaints form fill in your details and provide the necessary details such as:
  • The type of issue (e.g., food outlet, canteen, shop)
  • A brief description of the problem
  • Location (if applicable)
  • Any other relevant information
Be as specific as possible to help resolve the issue quickly.
5

Submit the Complaint
